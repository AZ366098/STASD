摘要
提供非審計服務是否一定會對會計師的獨立性或審計品質構成威脅，長久以來一
直存有爭議。本研究旨在從會計師抑制盈餘管理的角度，探討當會計師接受客戶審計
委任又額外執行內部控制專案審查此一重大非審計服務時，財務利益的誘因是否將減
損當年度的審計品質。實證結果發現，經由傾向分數配對法(propensity score matching
method)控制非審計服務內生性特徵後，在博達案前，會計師同時執行內部控制專案審
查時，其審計品質相對變得較差，即使改以 Heckman (1979)兩階段估計法控制非審計
服務內生性情況下，實證結論仍然維持不變，支持準租利益威脅獨立性的論點。

1. 前言
鑒於近年來審計失敗的案例頻傳，事件背後所引發的社會波瀾乃再次促使了管制
單位及學術專業對會計師獨立性問題的討論與檢視。在目前審計市場競爭激烈的環境
下，會計師既要保有審計公費的誘因又要切實秉持獨立性，本來就有一定程度的矛盾。
管制機關的立場係憂慮會計師在進行審計查核之餘再兼營非審計業務，將導致財務依
存度加深，恐損及會計師之超然獨立性，進而影響審計簽證之品質(Wallace 1987; Parkash
and Venable 1993; Firth 1997; Ahmed et al. 2006; Basioudis et al. 2008; Choi et al. 2006)1。

本研究旨在從會計師抑制盈餘管理的角度，探討內部控制專案審查此重大非審計
服務之提供是否一定會對審計品質構成威脅2。從我國於 2002 年 10 月 3 日修訂「證券
發行人財務報告編製準則」第 22 條，要求公司給付之非審計公費達到一定比例或金額
須揭露公費資料開始，即可看出主管機關對非審計服務之重視3。經檢視該揭露條件的
說明欄發現，現行符合重大非審計公費之揭露門檻者，高達 26%係客戶向會計師事務
所單獨購買內部控制專案審查服務所致。易言之，當會計師接受客戶審計委任又額外
執行內部控制專案審查時，因財務依賴程度擴大，是否將導致當年度的審計品質變得
更為低落，此乃為主管機關及學術專業亟欲得知的實證問題。
內部控制為公司治理重要一環，財政部證券暨期貨管理委員會（現已更名為行政
院金融監督管理委員會證券期貨局，文後簡稱證期局）2002 年 11 月 18 日修正發佈「公
開發行公司建立內部控制制度處理準則」，要求公開發行公司檢查內部控制制度設計
及執行的有效性，並做成內部控制聲明書。除了內部稽核職能外，內部控制檢查另有
訂定會計師專案審查之外部稽核機制，其目的不外乎借重會計師之專業，以強化公司
之內部控制。根據「會計師執行公開發行公司內部控制制度專案審查作業要點」，會
計師執行內控專案審查範圍包含「與財務報導有關之內部控制，及與保障資產安全，
使資產不致在未經授權之情況下取得、使用及處分有關之內部控制」。此外，按「公
開發行公司建立內部控制制度實施要點」第 11 點所規定之審查意見格式，公司得出具
內部控制聲明書，聲明其內部控制制度於設計上係屬有效，或不出具內部控制聲明書，
而由會計師直接查核其內部控制制度之設計是否有效（李建然、顏怡音 2005），即會
計師執行內控專案審查可由公司主動委託。不過，為輔導新公開發行公司或申請上市
上櫃（文後簡稱 IPO）公司建立有效內部控制制度，並使公司之利害關係人瞭解該公司
內部控制設計及執行是否有效，證期局另規定 IPO 公司須做成內部控制制度聲明書，

簽證客戶內部控制之良窳攸關會計師審計決策，從而影響審計品質。Pae and Yoo
(2001)建構分析性模型時即指出，審計品質是由會計師所投入的專業查核努力與客戶對
內部控制的投資所聯合決定，而會計師的查核努力與客戶的內部控制品質具有策略替
代(strategic substitutes)的性質，兩者間存在著負向關聯。會計師提供內部控制專案審查
此一非審計服務所產生的知識外溢效果，固然可降低審計服務成本以獲得更多的利潤
(Simunic 1984)，但會計師同時提供內部控制專案審查服務與審計服務也將因財務依存
度之提高而可能損及審計品質。此一疑慮特別是在簽證客戶內控品質較差的情況下，
會計師原應投入較多的查核努力俾維持良好審計品質，但卻可能基於聯合提供服務之
財務利益考量而減少應付出的查核成本，同時內部控制專案審查亦因此一經濟誘因而
喪失改善之契機。本研究資料顯示現行符合重大非審計公費之揭露門檻者，高達 26%
係客戶向會計師事務所單獨購買內部控制專案審查服務所致；另外，從有揭露內部控
制專案審查公費金額公司所提供之數據亦發現，該公費金額平均高達 113.5 萬4。因此，
在眾多非審計服務種類中，執行內部控制專案審查顯與審計品質關係密切5，本研究資
料亦顯示客戶向簽證會計師購買內部控制專案審查服務在非審計公費中所占的比例不
低，誠屬重大非審計服務性質。在維護財務報表品質與保障會計師權益之權衡下，是
否應限縮非審計服務的範圍，實有賴相關研究進一步的檢視與分析。

綜上所述，維持審計品質之訴訟威脅及聲譽損失的市場誘因機制在台灣的審計環
境不一定能成立，要檢測當會計師面對經濟準租與審計獨立的取捨時，究竟對審計品
質會造成何種影響，其實是財務依存度與市場誘因機制的權衡(Reynolds and Francis 2001;
Larcker and Richardson 2004)，而重大非審計服務因經濟誘因的效果強烈，恰好提供此
一適合的情境進行分析8。考量非審計服務帶來之財務利益是否造成會計師財務依存度
加深而威脅獨立性，抑或增加聲譽資本之投資而更能保有獨立性，一直是探討審計品
質議題持續關注的重點。另一方面，非審計服務產生之知識外溢效果對審計效率之助
益亦為政策管制考慮之重要因素，因此本研究乃以審計品質為探討主題，並特別檢視
特定種類非審計服務與審計品質間之關聯。
睽諸文獻除了對於提供非審計服務是否會降低會計師獨立性或影響盈餘品質的看
法未有共識外，對非審計服務範疇亦未曾深入剖析，多以整體非審計服務包裹方式驗
證其對審計品質的影響（李建然等 2003；楊炎杰、官月緞 2006；廖秀梅、洪筱雯
2010；官月緞、郭子建 2011）。然而，並非每一類非審計服務皆具有相同傷害審計品
質程度的疑慮(Kinney et al. 2004)，各類非審計服務對會計師專業性與獨立性的衝擊應
會有所不同，故其對審計品質所造成的影響效果未必能一概而論。鑒於內部控制專案
審查服務可對會計師專業性與獨立性同時產生影響，故相較於其他非審計服務項目而
言，聚焦在內部控制專案審查服務更可反映非審計服務問題之癥結。據此，本研究探
討查核會計師同時提供內部控制專案審查服務之議題，相信能裨益此類非審計服務是
否損害審計品質問題之釐清，並且在主管機關日後研議是否應限縮會計師對簽證客戶
提供內部控制專案審查此一非審計服務的管制上，提供若干政策制定上的參考證據。
本研究除了填補審計文獻上缺乏針對重大非審計服務類型影響審計品質證據的罅
漏外，並進一步在研究設計上尋求改進9。相較於過去的審計文獻，在處理提供非審計
服務不具外生性問題上，多半透過 Heckman (1979)兩階段估計法控制選擇性偏誤(Rud-
dock et al. 2006)。然而，前述校正方式有其限制，計量上亦無法降低根據可觀察變數做
選擇所衍生的選擇偏誤型態。本研究嘗試運用傾向分數配對法(propensity score matching
method)，在控制非審計服務內生性特徵下，針對會計師提供簽證客戶內部控制專案審
查，檢測該重大非審計服務是否真會影響審計品質的表現，此為本文另一研究貢獻。

2. 文獻探討與假說建立
本研究旨在探討簽證會計師對審計客戶額外提供內部控制專案審查之重大非審計
服務時，財務利益的誘因是否會影響會計師的審計品質。文獻回顧擬從非審計服務對
會計師獨立性之威脅及非審計服務與審計品質之關聯二方面來加以說明。
審定後的財務報表向來被視為是資本市場中相當重要的資訊來源，出具由專業審
計人員所審定的公司財務報表，實質上具有提升資訊品質與驗證資訊價值之功能(Watts
and Zimmerman 1986; Wallace 1987)。就審計扮演傳遞企業訊息價值的角度而言，審計
簽證的品質愈佳，愈能協助投資人精確評估公司經營價值(Titman and Trueman 1986; Wal-
lace 1987; Willenborg 1999; Behn et al. 2008)。然而，因繼續保有審計合約將可使現任會
計師享有成本優勢及賺取準租利益，使得現任會計師在面對客戶終止合約的威脅下，
難以完全堅守超然獨立的要求(DeAngelo 1981a)。當審計人員的獨立性遭受質疑，外部
資訊使用者在使用審計報告時所面對的資訊風險便會升高。
從財務依存度的角度來看，繼續維持委任關係所能獲致的準租利益若愈高，審計
客戶對會計師績效貢獻的程度就愈重要，如此愈會加深外界對會計師妥協獨立性的疑
慮(Trompeter 1994; Nelson et al. 2002)。Beck et al. (1988)便認為提供非審計服務對會計師
偵測重大誤述能力影響不大，但對會計師是否願意誠實報導重大誤述的影響較鉅。甚
且，非審計服務往往涉及管理職能，易使得會計師處於準管理者的角色(Frankel et al.
2002)。因此，查核會計師額外提供非審計服務將有損審計人員超然獨立性(Wallace 1987;
Parkash and Venable 1993; Firth 1997)。儘管財務利益的論點鏗鏘有力，但後續的研究對
於非審計服務之提供與會計師犧牲獨立性之間關聯性的證據仍相當的模糊。原因可能
在於維持審計品質之市場誘因機制會促使會計師評估審計失敗的成本，因而某種程度
會降低會計師妥協獨立性的誘因(Simunic 1984; Chung and Kallapur 2003; Ruddock et al.
2006)。因此，對於同時提供審計服務及非審計服務將損及審計品質的說法，目前仍然
有不少的文獻持保留態度(Ashbaugh et al. 2003; Chung and Kallapur 2003; Raghunandan et
al. 2003; Larcker and Richardson 2004; Ruddock et al. 2006)。
睽諸文獻發現，探討非審計服務與審計品質之間關聯性的文獻多半側重在盈餘品
質、盈餘反應係數與負面審計意見之簽發。就盈餘品質而言，當公司購買非審計服務

溢效果，將可強化會計師對客戶的瞭解與累積產業經驗，使得會計師得以更有效率方

1986; Simunic 1984; Kinney et al. 2004)。同時，非審計服務也可以增加會計師在聲譽資
本方面的投資(Dopuch et al. 2003)，長遠來看亦有助於審計品質之提升。實證上並未發

營有疑慮審計意見的機率(Craswell et al. 2002; Geiger and Rama 2003; DeFond et al.
2002)。

配對方法基本概念係針對聘請查核會計師對其內部控制進行專案審查的每家公司，
依據可觀察到的公司特徵變數是否相近為標準，在未聘請會計師對其內部控制進行專
案審查的公司中，尋找一個或多個樣本公司與之配對，使配對後兩類公司在特徵變數
上之既有差異減少甚至消失，在此情境下，再來檢測兩類企業審計品質的差異。實證
上執行傾向分數配對法時，第一步依據本文所選的公司特徵變數估計一機率函數，即
公司聘請會計師對其內部控制進行專案審查的機率函數，文獻上稱為傾向分數函數(pro-
pensity score function)，之後將個別公司（不論有無聘請會計師從事內部控制進行專案
審查）的特徵變數代入機率函數，得到所有樣本的估計機率或傾向分數。第二步再針
對購買內部控制專案審查服務公司組中之每一個樣本，依照傾向分數是否相近為依據，
從未購買內部控制專案審查服務公司組中尋找合適的配對樣本，俾形成本文的實驗組
樣本及控制組樣本，從而促使兩組樣本達到具有近似特徵變數分配的特性(the scored dis-
tributions are virtually identical)。

此外，鑒於博達案事件發生後，台灣的法律氛圍對會計師簽證責任的法律規範及
懲戒制度轉趨嚴格，會計師須擔負的行政責任及民事賠償責任也變得更為加重，因而
促使會計師對受查客戶的簽證行為有了明顯的改變，審計決策轉而更為保守（張瑞當
等 2009）。因此，本研究除了以全部樣本執行檢測外，後續亦將以博達案事件劃分樣
本，進一步比較會計師提供受查客戶內部控制專案審查服務對審計品質的影響，在博
達案前後是否有明顯的差異。
誠如前一小節所述，為控制審計客戶購買內部控制專案審查此一重大非審計服務
決策之自我選擇偏誤，本研究進一步經由傾向分數配對法將樣本予以配對(Boone et al.
2010; Lennox et al. 2012；沈中華、張元 2008），配對後之結果如表 1 panel B 所示。在
四種配對方法下，最後分別形成 983、981、991、106 筆觀察值19。至於在變數取得部
分，企業聘請會計師執行內部控制專案審查服務之資料係以台灣經濟新報社（Taiwan
Economic Journal，簡稱 TEJ）公費資料庫作為判斷依據，並搭配公開資訊觀測站所揭
露資訊，輔助判斷是否針對首次辦理公開發行或首次申請上市（櫃）公司執行內部控
制專案審查服務，其它估計異常應計數所需之資料及實證模型中控制變數之資料亦取
自 TEJ 資料庫。

4.5.1
內部控制專案審查服務需求屬性對審計品質的影響
按現行法令之規定，首次辦理公開發行及首次申請上市、上櫃公司（簡稱 IPO 公
司）必須委託會計師執行內部控制專案審查，如此一來相較於非 IPO 公司自願性購買
內控專案審查，IPO 公司內控專案審查需求便屬強制性。根據現有文獻，IPO 影響盈餘
品質方向並未有定論。Teoh et al. (1998)發現，公司初次上市櫃時，可能欲以高價出售
股票而有報導正的異常應計數之誘因，不過 Ball and Shivakumar (2008)指出，IPO 公司
因須面對廣大報表使用者，除法令規章之約束外，內部控制、外部審計、董事會、分
析師，甚至媒體亦會更為嚴密審視 IPO 公司之經營狀況，一旦出具不實資訊將遭受更
為嚴厲之懲處，故會計報導反而有可能趨向更為保守。為觀察內部控制專案審查需求
誘因不同是否會對審計品質產生不同的影響，本文乃進一步將樣本區分為強制性與自
願性兩群22。分群後之估計結果顯示：在強制性樣本中，會計師若同時執行內部控制專
案審查，反應出的審計品質相對較差（NASIC 之 p 值介於 0.0173 至 0.0702）；但在自
願性樣本部分，會計師額外執行內部控制專案審查則看不出明顯傷害審計品質的跡象，
此一差異性證據本文推測可能與 IPO 屬性有關。IPO 公司常出現更換會計師之情事。
考量會計師對新客戶之初始成本(start-up costs)較高，會計師通常會以低於初始成本的公
費延攬新客戶(low balling)，並希望透過將來的公費彌補此一損失，致使會計師可能為
獲取留任而配合管理當局進而影響其獨立性(DeAngelo 1981a; Reynolds and Francis
2001)，此時內部控制專案審查服務之提供也將因經濟依存度之提高，更易導致會計師
妥協獨立性，因而減損審計品質的現象相對更為明顯。此外，本研究另檢測 IPO 公司
與 Non-IPO 公司兩群樣本，比較在 IPO 之前（排除購買內部控制專案審查服務之影響）
盈餘品質之差異。平均數與中位數檢定結果顯示，IPO 公司前一年及前兩年盈餘品質並
未顯著較 Non-IPO 公司的盈餘品質來得差（四種情況下 p 值從 0.181 至 0.763)。另一方
面，亦發現 IPO 公司在上市櫃前一年從事更換會計師的比例(12.93%)確實較 Non-IPO
公司(8.76%)來得高（p 值為 0.039），意味著 IPO 樣本盈餘品質較差的證據，應是來自

4.5.3

與之前結果類似，實證結論並未改變。

4.5.5
以不同計量模型進行參數的估計與檢定
針對異常應計數的迴歸模型而言，Klein (2002)指出當因變數為|AA|若仍以 OLS 方
式進行估計，將使參數的估計變得較不具效率性，建議改以最大概似估計法(Maximum
Likelihood Estimation, MLE)執行參數的估計。其次，若因變數被分群為 AA+或 AA-時，
研究樣本已屬 truncated subsamples，實證上亦應以 truncated regression 取代傳統 OLS 執
行參數的估計(Myers et al. 2003; Boone et al. 2010)。經由上述不同計量模型重新進行參
數的估計後，研究結果顯示：不論是|AA|全體樣本或 AA+子樣本，NASIC 係數仍顯著為
正，而在 AA-子樣本中，NASIC 係數維持不顯著為負，估計結果與 OLS 結果差異不
大。
4.5.6
刪除極端值樣本所造成的影響
為了避免極端值(outlier)對實證結果產生的影響，本研究剔除各變數超過該變數正
負三倍標準差的觀察值，重新估計後研究結論並不受影響。
5. 結論與建議
提供非審計服務是否一定會對會計師的獨立性或報表品質構成威脅，長久以來一
直存有爭議，目前也尚無明確的結論形成。究其原由，研究設計上所建構的實證模型
缺乏足夠的檢定力，以致於未能檢測出非審計服務與審計品質的真正關聯性是原因之
一(Ruddock et al. 2006)；其次，忽視非審計服務的種類及其所衍生出財務利益的比重對
審計品質的不同影響，可能也是文獻上無法形成一致結論的另一原因（楊炎杰、官月
緞 2006）。鑒於是否應限制會計師兼營非審計服務及限制的範圍如何界定，此乃是主
管機關與審計供需者持續關切的專業議題，是故，本研究旨在進一步延伸非審計服務
的文獻，分別從研究設計的改進及聚焦特定的非審計服務（內部控制專案審查）著手，
探討當會計師接受客戶審計委任又額外執行內部控制專案審查時，財務利益誘因是否
將減損當年度的審計品質。
本文研究結果發現，經由配對樣本處理後，在博達案前接受內部控制專案審查服
務的客戶，其審計品質確實變得較差。迴歸分析結果支持當委託人聘請會計師執行內
部控制專案審查此一重大非審計服務時，確實會影響到當年度的審計品質，此一證據
即使改以 Heckman (1979)兩階段估計法來控制樣本選擇性偏誤後研究結論仍然維持不
變，印證了準租利益威脅獨立性的看法，然此準租利益傷害審計品質的現象在博達案
後並不明顯。
本研究與國內外已有文獻相較，預期主要貢獻在於提供了來自特定類別的非審計
服務影響審計品質的證據，藉由該證據也印證了在維持審計品質之市場誘因機制不強
的機構環境下，會計師的確會因為內部控制專案審查此重大非審計服務背後之經濟準
租而損及審計品質。再者，針對非審計服務內生性問題之研究設計改進上，本研究利
用傾向分數配對法以及會計文獻上慣用 Heckman (1979)兩階段估計法，控制來自可觀
察及不可觀察變數所導致之樣本選擇性偏誤，避免後者所估計的結果缺乏穩健性(infer-
ences are extremely fragile)的限制。
礙於資料蒐集的困難與配對方法研究設計上既有的問題，致使本研究難豁免有以

再者，雖然配對樣本的研究設計能有效降低依據可觀察變數做選擇所衍生的選擇性偏

計服務對審計品質的影響，實證結果未必可類推於其他不同類型之非審計服務。考量
不同類型非審計服務對會計師獨立性造成的影響有所差異，未來研究之延伸，若能取
得所有非審計服務項目完整的資料，深入探討各類型非審計服務與審計品質之關聯，
同時將影響各類非審計服務之結構性因素納入考量，應更能提高此一議題的完整性。

The Association between Substantial Non-Audit Services and
Audit Quality: Evidence from Special Audit of Internal
Control System†
Yi-Hsing Liaoa*
Teng-Sheng Sangb
Hsiu-Feng Linc
aChung Yuan Christian University
bChung Yuan Christian University
cTung-Hai University
Abstract
Whether non-audit services can impair auditor independence and audit quality has been
controversial for a long time. By utilizing abnormal accruals as an observable proxy for audit
quality, the purpose of this study is to examine whether audit quality is impaired when audi-
tors provide audit services and simultaneously conduct substantial non-audit services like
special audit of internal control system. Using propensity score matching method discussed by
Lennox et al. (2012) to control non-audit services self-selection bias, the empirical results
show that auditors compromise their audit quality if they provide the special audit of internal
control system to their clients concurrently before Procomp event. These results are still ro-
bust when the two-stage Heckman (1979) approachis adopted. That supports DeAngelo’s
(1981a) quasi-rents arguments.
Keywords: non-audit services, audit quality, propensity score matching method
Data Availability: All data are available from public sources.
1. Research Issue
Reports for frequent audit failures in recent years have reviews on auditor independence
among regulatory authorities and relevant academic fields. Amid the intensely competitive
environment of the current audit market, auditors must achieve a balance between the parad-
oxical demands of maintaining independence and upholding incentivesfor audit fees. The ma-
jor concern of regulatory authorities is that auditors may simultaneously engage in non-audit
services and auditing, which will increase financial dependency, jeopardize auditor indepen-
dence, and eventually compromises audit quality. However, scholars argue that considering
the joint guarantee that auditors are subjected to (i.e., the possibility of facing lawsuits and
loss of reputation), the actual incentives of auditor compromise may be overestimated.
Whether simultaneously providing audit and non-audit services impairs audit quality has yet
to be clarified; therefore, additional empirical evidence is required for further clarification.

This study adopted the perspective that auditors suppress earnings management to deter-
mine whether audit quality is impaired when auditors simultaneously provide auditing servi-
ces and conduct substantial non-audit services, such as that for special audit of internal con-
trol systems audit (hereafter NASIC). Currently, up to 26% of cases that qualify as substantial
non-audit services occur because clients separately purchase internal control audit services
from certified public accounting firms (hereafter CPA firm). Therefore, an empirical problem
is presented for regulatory authorities and academic fields on whether audit quality is compro-
mised because of increased financial dependency when auditors simultaneously provide audit
services and NASIC for clients.
Internal controls are an important segment of corporate governance. The Securities and
Futures Bureau, Financial Supervisory Commission, Executive Yuan (R.O.C) demands that
public companies ensure the effectiveness of the design and implementation of their internal
control systems (which are released as internal control audit report) to enhance the firm’s in-
ternal control by auditors’ expertise. A firm may either issue an internal control statement to
declare the effectiveness of its internal control system or hire auditors to directly audit the de-
sign effectiveness of its internal control systems (Li & Yan, 2005; i.e., the firm commissions
auditors to implement internal controls). However, to assist new public companies or firms
pending initial public offerings (IPOs) in establishing an effective internal control system, and
to assist stakeholders in understanding the design and execution effectiveness of the internal
control system, the Security and Futures Bureau mandates that IPO firms provide an internal
control statement (in addition to the other documents in the application package) to be review-
ed by auditors and inspected by the Securities and Futures Bureau.
In their efforts to develop an analytical model, Pae and Yoo (2001) report that audit qual-
ity is jointly determined by auditor effortand client investment in internal control systems.
Auditor effort and clients investment are strategically substitutable, and exhibit a negative re-
lation. Although audit service costs can be reduced and profits increased because of a knowl-
edge spillover effect that results from providing NASIC (i.e., a non-audit service; Simunic,
1984), audit quality may be compromised because of auditors’ financial dependency that re-
sults from concurrently providing NASIC and audit services to clients. Data collected in this
study show that a considerable proportion of non-audit fees comprise clients’ separate purcha-
ses NASIC from certified public accountants (CPAs), which qualifies as substantial non-audit
services. To maintain the quality of financial statements and protect auditors’ interests, further
reviews and analyses are needed to determine whether the scope of non-audit services should
be restricted. Amid the wide variety of non-audit services, a close relation exists between the
implementation of NASIC and audit quality. Data collected for this study indicate that among
cases that qualify as substantial non-audit services, up to 26% result from clients separately
purchasing NASIC from CPA firms. In addition, data provided by companies that have dis-
closed expenditure on NASIC indicate that their average expenditure on such services is NTD
$1.135 million. In summary, a close association exists between the implementation of NASIC
and audit quality among a wide variety of non-audit services.
The legal environment and organization type for CPA firms in Taiwan are structurally
different from those of the United States. In Taiwan, auditors are rarely sued or involved in
settlements because of audit failures, and thus assume lower legal costs than auditors in the U.
S. Moreover, Taiwan’s legal system stipulates that only individual auditors that are not em-
ployed in CPA firms can be subjected to lawsuits regarding audit responsibilities. In other
words, audit responsibilities are not assumed by entire CPA firms. As a result, internal self-
-----------------------------------------------------Page 42-----------------------------------------------------
﻿Y. Liao, T. Sang, & H. Lin/TaiwanAccountingReview9(1) (January 2013):111-158
152
regulatory mechanisms for mutual supervision within CPA firms are absent. In addition, Ta-
iwan’s disciplinary system for CPAs and investor protections are less rigorous than those of
the U.S. That is, economic losses resulting from poor audit quality are not necessarily greater
than the economic quasi-rent attainable from audit dependency; thus, driven by economic in-
centives, individual auditors are less likely to be restricted by concerns of reputation capital
loss.
When determining the influence of the trade-off between economic quasi-rents and audit
independence on audit quality, emphasis should be place on the balance between financial de-
pendence and market incentive mechanisms rather than the intense effects of economic incen-
tives for non-audit services to achieve a suitable context for analysis. Examining whether non-
audit services lead to auditors’ increased financial dependency (which in turn compromises
audit independence), or whether audit independence could be enhanced by increasing reputa-
tion capital investments, has been a continuous concern among audit quality issues. In addi-
tion, the benefits of the knowledge spillover effect, generated by non-audit services, has on
audit efficiency should be considered for policy stipulation. Therefore, in this study, audit
quality is explored by focusing on the correlation between specific non-audit services and au-
dit quality.
No consensus in extent literature exists regarding whether the provision of non-audit ser-
vices impairs auditor independence or compromises earnings quality. Furthermore, few stu-
dies have conducted in-depth analysis on the scope of non-audit services, other than examin-
ing the overall effect of non-audit services on audit quality. However, not all non-audit servi-
ces are equally harmful to audit quality. Different types of non-audit services exert varying
degrees of influence on auditors’ professionalism and independence; thus, the effects of non-
audit service on audit quality vary and cannot be generalized. In light of the possible simulta-
neous influence of NASIC on auditors’ occupational practices and independence, focusing on
the NASIC may better reveal the significance of the problems regarding non-audit services.
Consequently, this study address the issues that arise when auditors simultaneously provide
audit and NASIC to determine whether non-audit services impair audit quality, and this study
provides references for future policy making regarding whether restrictions should be im-
posed to limit auditors from providing non-audit services (e.g., special audit of internal con-
trol system, NASIC).
In addition to compensate for lacking literature regarding the effect of substantial non-
audit services on audit quality, this study seeks to improve the research design. By addressing
the non-exogenous provisions of non-audit services, most previous studies have controlled the
selection bias using Heckman’s (1979) two-stage estimation method. However, such correct-
ive methods are limited, and are econometrically incapable of reducing selection bias derived
from observable variables. By employing the propensity score matching method and control-
ling the endogenous characteristics of non-audit services, this study examined whether sub-
stantial non-audit services, such as the provision of NASIC, impair audit quality. This is an ad-
ditional contribution of this study.
As hypothesized, the results of propensity score matching show that clients receiving
NASIC prior to the Procomp scandal exhibited compromised audit quality. Results for the re-
gression analysis support the hypothesis that annual audit quality is compromised when sub-
stantial non-audit services (e.g., NASIC purchased separately by the clients) are conducted.
These results remained robust when the two-stage Heckman (1979) approach is adopted to
control the influence of selection bias. Therefore, the hypothesis that quasi-rent interests imp-

2. Hypothesis
Numerous studies pertaining to the correlation between non-audit services and audit
quality have focused on earnings quality, earnings response coefficients, and negative audit
opinion disclaimers. Regarding earnings quality, when the purchased non-audit services are of
a higher quality, the extent of earnings management increases, and the published earnings
figures are more likely to precisely meet or slightly beat analysts’ forecasts, indicating that
providing non-audit services impair auditors’ independence and reduce the quality of finan-
cial statement`. For investors, a greater non-audit fee reduces corporate earnings response co-
efficients, which signifies that investors perceive that an exceedingly high proportion of non-
audit services are not conducive to audit quality. Finally, regarding audit opinion, the provi-
sion of non-audit services reduces the probability of issuing qualified or going-concern audit
report. These findings are consistent with that of DeAngelo (1981a) regarding quasi-rent.
However, other studies have argued that under the constraints of incentive mechanisms, insuf-
ficient supportive evidence exists for the impairing effect of non-audit services on audit qual-
ity. Several studies have contended that the knowledge spillover effect arising from simu-
ltaneously undertaking audit and non-audit services enhances the auditors’ understanding of
the clients and enables the accumulation for industry experience; thus, enabling auditors to
provide services to clients with improved efficiency. Consequently, the quality of financial
statements is improved. In addition, non-audit services may increase auditors’ investment in
reputation capital, which enhances audit quality in prospect. The diversity of opinions regar-
ding whether the provision of non-audit services impairs auditor independence or earnings
quality can be attributed to various factors, such as national and cultural differences, differing
sample characteristics, and variations in research design. Regarding market incentive mecha-
nisms, types and scopes of non-audit services are critical for determining audit quality. There-
fore, this study hypothesizes that despite the less rigorous liabilities in Taiwan’s legal system
compared to that of the United States, the provision of substantial non-audit services impairs
auditor independence, which in turn compromises earnings quality. Hence, this study pro-
poses the following hypothesis:
Hypothesis: The provision of special audit of internal control systems compromises audit
quality compared to situations where no special audit of internal control systems
are involved.

sensitivity testsare performed as follows:
4.1 The influence of the attributes for NASIC demands on audit quality
The study samples are further divide into a mandatory and voluntary group. The results
after grouping show that in the mandatory sample, auditors involved in NASIC exhibited rela-
tively poor quality (i.e., the p value of the NASIC ranges from 0.0173 to 0.0702); in the volun-
tary sample, auditors involved in NASIC exhibit no obvious signs of compromising audit
quality. This difference may be related to the IPO attributes, because auditor replacements are
frequent in IPO companies. Considering the high start-up costs for new clients, auditors gen-
erally attract new clients by offering low-cost services, and the incurred loss may be recupe-
rated in subsequent deals. Under such circumstances, auditors may be forced to cooperate
with the administrative authorities to maintain their tenure; thus, compromising their indepen-
dence. IfNASIC are provided concurrently, the increased financial dependence further com-
promises audit independence, resulting in a more significant impairment of audit quality.

We estimate parameters using the maximum likelihood estimation and truncated re-

To avoid the influence of outliers on the empirical results, this study excludesthe obser-

This study provides extended research on non-audit services. To improve research design
with a focus on specific non-audit services (i.e., audits of internal controls), we investigated
whether economic incentives impair the audit quality when auditors concurrently accept audit
appointments from clients and perform special non-audit services prior to the Procomp scan-
dal, and find that their audit quality is compromised. The regression results indicate that the
audit quality is compromised when substantial non-audit services (e.g., special audit of inter-
nal control systems purchased separately by the clients) are conducted, confirming the prop-
osition that quasi-rent interests impair audit independence. However, this impairment is insig-
nificant after the Procomp scandal.
